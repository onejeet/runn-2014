RUNN 2014

RUNN is the inter-collegiate sports festival of College of Engineering Roorkee.For this year, RUNN is commencing from 12th April to 16th April . The fest includes Basketball (M&W) Cricket (M), Volley-Ball(M), Badminton (M&W) and Table-Tennis(M). It offers a platform for students from all over India to showcase their talent and compete with the best upcoming sport persons in the country. We,at RUNN, have always aimed to bring about a rise in competition level among the youth, empowering the masses with the torch of social awareness and plethora of entertainment.The fest includes Basketball (M&W) Cricket (M), Volley-Ball(M),Badminton (M&W) and Table-Tennis(M).

History

Runn began its journey in 2012 with cricket. After the great accomplishment of the inaugral season, runn turned into a inter-collegiate fest in 2013. In 2013, we took a leap in sports by introducing basketball, Table-tennis and volleyball.this version draws participations from over 30 colleges and 500 students from NCR and UTU. This year introduction of badminton excels Runn even more. The fest completes 3 years of success in 2014 and we promise you a bigger and a better Runn than ever before.

General Rules for RUNN 2014

• Only a bonafide student, who is currently officially enrolled/registered for a degree in the university/ its colleges or institutes, whose status is recognised an appropriate authority, which is of a minimum duration of 2 years and whose examination is conducted by the university shall be eligible to participate in the tournament.
• Ph.d. students will be eligible to participate only in terms of the university’s rules that they are regarded to be a bonafide student and full fill the conditions.
• All students while participating in the tournament shall full fill the following conditions:
• Only students who are less than 28 years of age as on 1st july of the academic year in which the tournament is held, can participate.
• A student who is employed on full time basis will not be eligible for participating in the tournament.
• A student shall not represent more than one college/institute.

Disqualifications

• Any disqualification of the sportsperson on the ground of ineligibility will result in automatic scratching of his team, the team may be debarred from participating in the tournament to be held in following year, if required.
• A disqualified sportsperson on the basis of ineligibility shall not be permitted to participate in the tournament in next year, if required.
• In case of incident of violence during sporting competition, the organizing committee have powers to cancel your participation in the tournament.
• The team will be disqualified if any of the player of team is found with any drugs or caught in case of doping, smoking, drinking.
